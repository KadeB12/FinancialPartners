Why We Do What We Do

We love business and the entrepreneur in particular. The entrepreneur is tough to get capital for. The HARD DEAL (a deal with hair on it like bad credit, new to the market, low asset owners) is the toughest to get capital for. It takes experience and knowledge in order to be successful consistently. This, in turn, is also a great opportunity for our investors to demand higher returns for the risk they are assuming. It’s not SHARK, its symbiotic. The borrower gets the badly needed capital (debt/equity on any mix) and their project completed profitably (or else we wouldn’t do it) and the Investor gets the alternative investment they are seeking based on the risk they are willing to assume. It’s our pleasure to know the players and set the team up accordingly!
